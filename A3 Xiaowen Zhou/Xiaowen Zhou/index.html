Our Story

Next Coffee Adventure Awaits At Phoenix Park Cafe!

Our Cafe first opened its doors in 2004, quickly becoming a beloved local gathering place. Over the past decade, we have proudly served the community, offering a warm and welcoming atmosphere for all who visit. We strive to be a staple in the lives of our patrons, providing great food, drinks, and a friendly environment. Whether you are looking for a cozy spot to unwind alone or enjoy time with family and friends, our cafe has something for everyone.

We boast a diverse menu that includes gourmet pastries and sandwiches, homemade soups and salads, and an assortment of sweet treats such as cakes and ice cream. We also offer a variety of drinks, from coffee and tea to beer, wine, and sparkling beverages. Our commitment to quality extends to our use of fresh, often organic ingredients to prepare every dish.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Phoenix Park Cafe is conveniently located in the heart of the Malvern East at 22-28 Rob Roy Rd, near the bustling Phoenix Park Community Centre. You can find us just a few blocks from Chadstone Shopping Centre, making it an ideal spot for a relaxing meal after a stroll.

  • Yes, we love your furry friends! Dogs are welcome at our outdoor seating area. Please keep them leashed and under control during your visit.

  • Phoenix Park Cafe is open Monday through Friday from 7:00 AM to 9:00 PM, and on weekends from 8:00 AM to 10:00 PM. Join us for a meal, a quick snack, or a leisurely drink any day of the week.

  • Yes, we cater to all dietary needs. Our menu includes a variety of vegan and gluten-free dishes. Please ask our staff for recommendations or check our menu online for specific dietary options.

  • Absolutely! We accept reservations for both small groups and large events. To book a table or inquire about hosting an event, please call us at 0400 929 050 or visit our website to fill out an event booking form.
